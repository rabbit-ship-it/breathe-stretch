A calmer day,
20 minutes at a time.

Breathe & Stretch is a free Chrome extension that gives you a gentle 30-second break every 20 minutes.

⬇ Add to Chrome — it's free

What's inside

⏱

Smart 20-min timer

A calming full-screen overlay appears every 20 minutes with a breathing or stretching tip.

🤝

Meeting-aware

Smart Pause detects Zoom, Google Meet, and Teams tabs and delays your break by 5 minutes.

🔔

Tibetan singing bowl

A synthesised bell tone plays softly at the start of each break to signal your moment of calm.

🌸

Zen Garden

Watch your garden grow as you complete breaks. A tiny seed blossoms into full bloom over time.

💬

Your affirmations

Add your own personal reminders and they'll mix into the break rotation alongside the defaults.

✨

Custom toolbar icon

Choose from a set of calm emoji icons for your toolbar so the extension always feels like yours.

🔒 Zero data collected — no accounts, no analytics, no network requests. Privacy policy →
